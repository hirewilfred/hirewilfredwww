Frequently asked

Will Wilfred try to replace my services?

No. We're explicit about this in every partner agreement: Wilfred staffs AI specialists for tasks; you own the strategy, the relationship, and the human work. If your client needs a senior CPA, a recruiter, or a fractional CIO, that's still you.

How does deal registration work?

You submit the account through the partner portal before introduction. Once registered, the account is locked to your partnership for 12 months — we will not engage them directly or through another partner during that window.

What's the difference between referral and white-label?

Referral: client signs with Wilfred directly, you earn 20% MRR for life. We do the onboarding and support; you stay in the loop. White-label: client signs with you, you mark up Wilfred's wholesale rate, you handle Tier-1 — we handle infrastructure and Tier-2. Up to 30% margin.

How long until I get paid?

Referral commission is paid on the 15th of the following month, in CAD, by EFT. No quarterly batching, no minimums.

Can I see Wilfred before I commit?

Yes. Every partner gets a free, unlimited sandbox environment to run any of the agents against test data for 30 days. No card required.
