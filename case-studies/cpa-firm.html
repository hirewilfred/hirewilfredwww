The setup

28-person accounting firm serving small-business clients. Junior bookkeepers were spending 70% of their time on transaction categorization and reconciliation — the work nobody wants to do. The firm was losing junior staff faster than they could train them.

What they did

Deployed Quinn (Bookkeeper) across 40+ active client QuickBooks Online files. The arrangement: Quinn does the daily reconciliation; junior bookkeepers review Quinn's work and handle exceptions; senior accountants do tax, advisory, and CFO services.

The firm took on 14 new SMB clients in 9 months without a single new hire. Junior bookkeeper retention improved (the work is now interesting). Margin per client went up ~18%. The firm now markets "AI-assisted bookkeeping" as a tier of service.
