How we connect

Native API first. Wherever a vendor offers an official API, that's what we use — read-write with scoped permissions, audit-logged on every action, reversible by your admin in one click.

Browser automation as a fallback. For legacy tools without an API, Wilfred can drive a secure headless browser on your behalf, logged into your account via your SSO. Same audit log, same human-approval gates.

Your credentials never leave Canada. Tokens are stored encrypted in our Canadian Azure tenant with customer-managed keys on the Sovereign tier.
